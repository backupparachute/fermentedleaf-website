A work-in-progress index of lounges, smoke-friendly hotel bars, and
members' rooms that reward going out of your way for them. We add to
this slowly — one city at a time, after someone on staff has been.

## Coming soon

- **Madrid** — an evening's walk through the last great smoking
  cafés
- **Havana** — complicated, but unavoidable; the etiquette and the
  rooms that still matter

If you know of a room that belongs on this list, the editor would like
