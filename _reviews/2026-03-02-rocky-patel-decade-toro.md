Bread crust and black tea — homely flavors, honestly delivered. The
Decade Toro is a workaday cigar in the best sense of the phrase. It
punches well above its modest asking price.

Not a contemplative smoke so much as a companionable one. Put it in
rotation for Tuesday evenings.
