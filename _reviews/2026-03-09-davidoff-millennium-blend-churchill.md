Toasted almond on the cold draw. Lit, the Millennium Churchill settles
into a register that is unmistakably Davidoff — refined, even, and
completely unwilling to show off.

A whisper of orange peel arrives at the midpoint and lingers as a bright
counterpoint to the nuttier base. The combustion is remarkably even. A
cigar that rewards attention without ever demanding it.
