A near-perfect draw. The Exclusivo opens on espresso and aged cedar and
never strays far from that register, which is exactly the point — it is a
cigar that knows what it is.

Construction is, as always with Padrón, impeccable. The burn is razor
straight through all three thirds. The finish is long and quiet and
asks, politely, for a second cigar.
